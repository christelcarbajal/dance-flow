The Thunderboom Records Foundation is committed to facilitate responsible development and application of artificial intelligence (AI) in the music industry. To reach this goal, Thunderboom Records develops computer-generated robot artists that do not only write, sing and produce their own music, but also generate their own appearance. We love to collaborate with anyone willing to contribute to the field of technology, music and design.
